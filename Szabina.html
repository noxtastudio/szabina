Twelve places, and the weather they left inside of me.

A slow drive through the American Southwest, ending at a motocross track in Belgium and a quiet pier in California. The high light and the low light. The sleep, and the lack of it. The canyons that asked questions I could not answer.

I am not a photographer. I am a person with a camera and a small habit of stopping the car. If something here found you, write back — the rest lives, mostly, on Instagram.

— Szabina.
